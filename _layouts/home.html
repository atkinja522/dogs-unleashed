Expert dog walking & behaviour services

We’re Dogs Unleashed, a Lancaster based dog walking & behaviourist business run by Elise Atkin

Get in touch

Hi, I’m Elise and I founded Dogs Unleashed in 2016 to fulfil my dream of providing the dogs of Lancaster with enriching walks amongst the beautiful local area.

Dog Walks

I walk dogs in small carefully chosen groups depending on their abilities and needs, to make the walk harmonious and enjoyable for all. I plan for walks to be a combination of fun adventures, training, sniffing, confidence building and decompression depending on each dog’s needs each walk.

Walk Locations

Whether a quiet sniff through the woods, an exciting adventure down the river or a mix of both, all walk locations are determined by the group’s needs and abilities. I also carefully consider walk locations to minimize the time your dog will spend travelling in the van.

Walk Times

I offer the choice of morning or afternoon walks between the hours of 10.30AM-4.30PM to suit you and your dog’s needs. All walks are 90 minutes so you can be assured that your dog has had a long stimulating experience.

Join our walks

If you would like a regular slot for your dog to join our adventures and your dog is social, over 12 months (18months for large breeds), fit enough for a 90min walk, lives in Lancaster, vaccinated, up to date with flea, worms, tick treatments/titre tested and neutered please contact me for more information.

I am reliable, fully insured and I collect and drop off your dog at your home in my crated van.

Please note: I do not walk dogs on an ad hoc basis. I walk in all weathers so your dog must be happy to play out in the rain.
