Expert dog walking services in Lancaster

Dogs Unleashed is a Lancaster based dog walking business run by Elise Atkin

Get in touch

Hi, I’m Elise and I founded Dogs Unleashed in 2016 to fulfil my dream of providing the dogs of Lancaster with enriching walks amongst the beautiful local area.

Dog Walks

All walks are designed to encourage natural behaviors like sniffing, exploring, and playing, promoting mental and emotional well-being while keeping your dog mentally and physically enriched.

Each group is thoughtfully curated to foster confidence, positive experiences, and problem-solving skills, ensuring a harmonious and enjoyable walk for every dog.

Walk Locations

Whether it’s a quiet sniff through the woods, an exciting adventure by the river, or a mix of both, walk locations are chosen based on the group’s needs and abilities.

I carefully select locations to keep travel time in my comfortable, crated van to a minimum, ensuring your dog spends more time enjoying the walk and less time on the road.

Walk Times

I offer morning and afternoon walks between 11 AM and 4 PM to suit both you and your dog’s needs.

Each walk lasts 90 minutes, providing plenty of time for a stimulating and enriching experience.

Join our walks

To join our regular walking group, dogs must meet the following criteria:

  • Location: Live in Lancaster
  • Social Compatibility: Be friendly and comfortable around other dogs
  • Age:
    Over 12 months for small/medium breeds
    Over 18 months for large/XL breeds
  • Fitness: Fit and healthy enough for a 90-minute walk
  • Health & Safety:
    Neutered
    Vaccinated or Titre tested
    Treated regularly for worms, fleas, and ticks

Please note: I do not offer one-off walks. A commitment to regular walks, with a minimum of one walk per week, is required to join our walks.

If your dog is suitable to join us, please get in touch to arrange a meet and greet.
